# About
I am an experimental physicist specializing in the development of superconducting qubit chips and integrated hardware-software control systems for cryogenic digital microelectronics. My career bridges cutting-edge research at CERN’s Large Hadron Collider—where I developed novel detectors for the CMS experiment—with leadership as a Principal Investigator for federal grants and a peer-review journal editor. I bring deep technical proficiency in Python, C++, and machine learning, alongside extensive hands-on expertise in cryogenics, instrument control, and particle detection systems.
